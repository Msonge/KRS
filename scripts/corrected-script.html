Effect of elephant damage, biotic & abiotic factors on the growth rate of trees in Serengeti

Charles Msonge

November 2019

This script is about manipulating the elephant damage on tree dataset inorder to see the effect of various attributes such as fire, rainfall, site, elephant damage, etc on the growth rate of trees in the serengeti savannah ecosystem.
